About my work:



I create art that ellicts feelings of nostalgia while also looking towards the future. Because of this, I tend to incorporate retro visuals or even music into my pieces - typically from the early 2000's to the late 80's. Additionally, I enjoy exploring religious concepts as well as tranquility and escapism. My interests as an artist lie in the digital field: 3D Modeling, Game art and development, and overall cinematic experiences. My inspirations for my artwork come from my own childhood and from my strong interest in science fiction and technology. My recent work has been largely inspired by Vaporwave: a genre that focuses on 80's and 90's imagery and sound.
